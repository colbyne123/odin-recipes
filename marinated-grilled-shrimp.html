Delicious Marinated Grilled Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 lbs fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 6 each skewers

Steps

  1. Stir together olive oil, tomato sauce, garlic, and red wine vinegar in a large bowl
  2. Season with basil, salt, and cayenne pepper
  3. Add shrimp to the bowl; stir until evenly coated
  4. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour, stirring once or twice
  5. Preheat the grill to medium heat. Lightly oil the grate
  6. Thread shrimp onto skewer, piercing once near tail and once near head
  7. Discard marinade
  8. Cook shrimp on the preheated grill until opaque, 2 to 3 minutes per side
